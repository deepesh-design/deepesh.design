A Little About Me.

🏠  Where I'm from

I’m Deepesh Kamalapuram, born and raised in Southern India. I discovered my creativity early on, despite being told it wouldn’t lead to a successful career.

👨‍🎓 How it started

I enrolled in engineering without a clear path, but a like-minded friend reignited my creativity which helped me land a Product Design role at Milkieway Inc.

✴️ Turning Point

Working as a UX Motion Designer at Adobe redefined how I approach design, focusing on meaningful, user-centered experiences.

➡️ How's it going

I would say it's a wonderful journey so far and will be grateful to everyone who believed in me for my skills.

🤩 What's Next

Looking for an impactful and creative team to work with and invent the future of Digital Experience.
